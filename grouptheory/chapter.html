What is a group theory?
Group theory is a theory that studies a special creature in mathematics called group. But what is a group you might say? There are few axioms that define a group. Here are axioms of a group: Here are axioms of a group:

\[(G, \circ ) \;\;\;\;\;\;\;\; \circ : G \times G \to G\] 1)Identity member: \[\exists e \in G : \forall g \in G: eg=ge=g\] 2)Inverse member: \[\forall g \in G: \exists g^{-1} \to g g^{-1} = g^{-1} g = e\] 3)Associativity: \[\forall g,h,l \in G: g \circ (h \circ l) = (g \circ h ) \circ l\]
